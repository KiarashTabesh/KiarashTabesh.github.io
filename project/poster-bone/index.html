This team-work project was started in Sep 2020 in the course of “Methods and Tools for Systematic Innovation” under the supervision of Professor Gaetano Cascini and the collaboration of Rold Company. Our goal was to develop a concept to reduce the energy consumption of refrigerators. After we developed the conceptual design using the systematic methods, Professor Mario Guagliano supervised us to develope the detailed mechanical disign using the knowledge we learnt from the course of “Advanced Mechanical Design”.

We took advantage of inventor to prepare the CAD model and the drawings, and Abaqus CAE for FEM assessments. After considering DOX, and after performing different mechanical analysis using the finite element method, we can claim that the project is now ready for prototyping.

Our project was recognized as one of the best student projects by Rold company in Jan 2021, and the project got a full mark in the course of Advanced Mechanical Design. I would like to thank my colleagues for the grate collaboration and team-work: Alessandra Panerai, Edison Shehu, and Lorenzo Serventi.
